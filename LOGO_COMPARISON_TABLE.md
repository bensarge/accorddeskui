## Accessibility Considerations

### High Contrast
- Inverted Dark (white on dark)
- Outlined Border (clear boundaries)
- Large sizes (300px, 250px, 220px, 200px)

### Low Contrast (Use with caution)
- Subtle Opacity (60%)
- Document Watermark (20%)
- Grayscale (before hover)

### Motion Considerations
- Pulse Animation (can be disabled with prefers-reduced-motion)
- Fade in/out (loading screen)
- Hover effects (should not be essential)

## Quick Decision Matrix

**Need maximum impact?** → Hero Extra Large (300px)  
**Need minimal space?** → Mobile App Header (44px)  
**Need dark mode?** → Inverted Dark or Soft Glow  
**Need animation?** → Pulse or Loading Screen  
**Need responsive?** → CSS Clamp or Breakpoint-based  
**Need interactive?** → Grayscale Hover or 3D Tilt  
**Need subtle?** → Opacity or Watermark  
**Need professional?** → Print Document or Email Header  
**Need modern?** → Gradient Overlay or 3D Tilt  
**Need classic?** → Sepia Vintage or Outlined Border
